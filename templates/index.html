FAQ

  • What is the release date?

    Pre-sale starts on Monday 14th of March, 11 AM ET for 24 hours for Whitelist holders. Registration to the public raffle will start Tuesday 15th of March at 11 AM ET for 24 hours. The public sale - for the raffle’s winners - starts Monday 6th of June at 11 AM ET for 24 hours.

  • What is the difference between Pre-sale, public raffle and public sale?

    The Pre-sale is open only to Whitelist spot holders. To avoid high minting gas fees, we will do a raffle and select a limited number of wallets that can participate in the mint at the public sale, out of all the ones that registered. Only wallets that are in our raffle will be eligible to mint on our public sale, on the basis of First Come, First Served.

  • How many CryptoDragonies can I mint?

    Each wallet can mint up to 5 CrypoDragonies.

  • What is the mint price?

    The mint price is 0.08 ETH (+Gas fee)

  • How do I register for the public-sale raffle?

    Visit our website on Tuesday 15th of March at 11 AM ET, connect your wallet, and click ‘Enter Raffle’. Only wallets with Mint Price + GAS fees will be eligible to enter the raffle.

  • How do I know if I won the public-sale raffle?

    If luck is on your side and your wallet address wins in the raffle, it will allow you to mint when the public sale starts, still on a First Come, First Served basis. To know if you won, you can connect your wallet on Wednesday 16th of February at 11 AM ET to check.

  • How can I avoid being scammed?

    To limit this as much as possible, make sure to follow only our official Discord, Twitter and Instagram channels. WE WILL NEVER DIRECT MESSAGE You. For more information on staying safe: Check our #scam-warning channel on our Discord server. And use only official links.
